Behringer AoIP (Dante and WSG) module relocation


WING firmware 3.1 enables the WING-DANTE expansion card to operate with either a
Dante or a Waves SoundGrid (WSG) audio-over-IP module.

   1. Install the desired AoIP module in the expansion card slot, then insert the
      expansion card into the console.

   2. Hold the UTILITY button while powering on the console.
   3. In the CONSOLE OPTIONS dialog, enter the corresponding string:
         •   For Dante: CRD-WDANTE
         •   For WSG: CRD-WDWSG
   4. Restart the console.
